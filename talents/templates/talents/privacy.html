1. Introduction

Welcome to TalentHub ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. When you visit our website and use our services, you trust us with your personal information. We take that privacy seriously.

This privacy notice describes how we might use your information if you use our freelance marketplace platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website.

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and profile photographs.
  • Payment Data: We may collect data necessary to process your payment if you make purchases, such as your payment instrument number (such as a credit card number).
  • Professional Data: Skills, education history, portfolio links, and work experience provided in your profile.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use personal information collected via our website for a variety of business purposes described below:

  • To facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • To enable user-to-user communications (e.g., messaging between Clients and Freelancers).
  • To manage user accounts and maintain the security of the platform.
  • To send administrative information to you regarding service updates or changes to our terms.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations.

Note: We do NOT sell your personal data to third-party advertisers. Your data is used strictly to operate the TalentHub marketplace.

5. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.
