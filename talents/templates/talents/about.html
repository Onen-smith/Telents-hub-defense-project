Our Story

Why we started TalentHub

For too long, skilled Nigerian professionals have faced barriers to entry in the global market—payment issues, trust deficits, and platform bias.

TalentHub was born from a final year project with a simple goal: Create a localized yet world-class platform where trust is built-in. We use secure escrow payments and strict verification to ensure that when work is done, payment is guaranteed.
